JAPAN


Explore!

Japan, an island country in East Asia, is full of beautiful places to visit. From the bustling city of Tokyo with its high-tech Akihabara district, to the ancient temples of Kyoto, there's something for everyone. Osaka is known for its modern architecture, nightlife and hearty street food. The northernmost island, Hokkaido, is a must-visit for nature lovers and is famous for its ski areas and hot springs. Each city offers a unique experience and a glimpse into the rich culture and history of Japan.
